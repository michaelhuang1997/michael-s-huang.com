Podverse

Project Overview

Podverse turns individual scholarly papers into short, listenable episodes. Using Google NotebookLM (Notebook ML), I generate guided audio summaries, key insights, and brief critiques. Each episode links the audio to the original paper (DOI/preprint) and a one-paragraph synopsis.

How it works

  1. Import the paper (PDF/URL) into NotebookLM and scaffold questions.
  2. Co-generate a narrative script (context, findings, critique, why-it-matters).
  3. Auto-narrate and export audio, lightly edited for clarity.
